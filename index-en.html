Kyivstar’s SMS for Million campaign gives away the grand prize of UAH 1,000,000 on Sunday, March 20th 2016 to the top scorer.


Want more? From November 23th 2015 to March 16th 2016, twice a week (Monday 00:00:00 to Wednesday 23:59:59 and from Thursday 00:00:00 to Sunday 23:59:59) the top scorer gets the prize of UAH 100,000 instantly without a draw.

How to participate

  • Send YES to 900 and join the SMS campaign (cost of 1 SMS – UAH 7.50 including VAT and PF). Shortly after, you will receive a welcome message introducing to you the game and your first 20 points

  • Right after, you will receive a message with one question and two possible answers, the one is correct and the other is wrong. You will have to send the number of the correct answer (either 1 or 2) to number 900

  • In the following message, you will be informed that the last question you answered is either correct or not, the points earned and the next question is coming

  • Continue to answer questions correctly, gather points and get closer to the cash prizes

FAQ

What is the duration of the campaign?

The Campaign "SMS for million" starts on the 23th of November 2015 at 00:00:01 and ends on 20th of March 2016 at 23:59:59. The total duration of Campaign is 119 days.

What is the number for participating in the campaign?

To participate, send an SMS with the word "YES" to the number 900 (the cost of one SMS - 7,50 UAH. (Including VAT and PF)).

How can I participate?

It is very easy to join the campaign "SMS for million”. You just need to:


1Send an SMS-message with the word "YES" to the number 900 (the cost of one SMS - 7,50 UAH. (Including VAT and PF)).


2You will then receive a welcome message and the first 20 points.


3Next, you have to answer campaign questions, by sending SMS. Questions and answer choices will be given in the SMS. Choose your answer and send a number (1 or 2), which corresponds to the chosen answer to the number 900 (the cost of one SMS - 7,50UAH (including VAT and PF)).


4With each answer you will receive points. Even if you answer incorrectly, the given points will still increase your score.


Continue to answer questions and gather points to get closer to victory! You can always win the grand prize - 1 million UAH!

What are the prizes?

Weekly Prizes:
– 2 prizes – 100 000 UAH each – will be given to two participants of the campaign, accordingly to the Terms and Conditions of the campaign.



Grand Prizes:
- 1 million UAH will be awarded to the winner of the campaign, according to the Terms and Conditions of the campaign.

How will I know that I won an extra prize?

You will be informed via SMS during the campaign.

Can a winner of a prize win again another prize?

Each participant can receive one weekly prize, and become the owner of the Grand Prize. Thus, you can get 2 prizes - 1 from each category.

What is the service Subscription?

By activating the service “Subscription”, you will receive additional points on daily basis, that will be added to your score and participate in the campaign.

How can I register in the service “Subscription”?

Send a free SMS-message with the word «OK» to number 901 to order and activate the service "Subscription". If activation is successful, you will get 1 free week, during which you will be awarded with additional points. Thanks to them, you will be able to accumulate more points and get closer to winning the Grand Prize and other prizes!

Service “Subscription” is available for free?

The free period lasts 7 days after activation of the service "Subscription". During this period, you will get free points to increase the score to become a winner.

What is the charge of the service “Subscription” after the free period?

7 days after the activation of Subscription Service cost will become UAH 2.50 (including VAT and surcharges to the PF) till 20.03.2016 inclusive (unless you unsubscribe from the service earlier). The service can also be extended and all the participants of the “Subscription” service will be informed via an SMS and on the campaign’s website.

How to opt-out from service “Subscription”?

You can disable the service "Subscription" by sending a free SMS with the word "STOP" to number 901.

I no longer want to receive sms messages with questions.

Send a free SMS with the word "STOP" to number 991 and you will no longer receive sms-messages with questions. Your points will no change, and you can still win prizes. You can resume participation in the campaign by sending an SMS-messages to number 900.

I am not a subscriber of Kyivstar, can I participate in the Campaign?

Participants of the campaign can only be subscribers of "Kyivstar". If you do not have a Kyivstar number, purchase a prepaid starter package "Kyivstar» ® and take part in the Campaign. The first thing you need to do - send an SMS-message with the word «OK» to the number 900 (the cost of one SMS - 7,50 UAH. (Including VAT and PF)).

How can I learn how many points I have collected so far?

To learn your collected points, please send the keyword “score” to 991, free of charge.

How can I change the language of the messages?

For Ukrainian language, please send the keyword “UKR” to 991, free of charge. For Russian language, please send the keyword “RUS” to 991, free of charge.
